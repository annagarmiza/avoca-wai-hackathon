AVOCA

  • Environment

    Their fast fashion model harms the environment due to frequent turnover and trend-focused production.

    Initiatives like garment reuse and recycled packaging are positive, but they don't minimize textile waste during production.

  • Labour Conditions

    Production in countries with medium labor abuse risks raises concerns.

    While somewhat transparent, Zara lacks progress in ensuring a living wage across its supply chain, despite substantial profits.

  • Animal Welfare

    Zara bans fur and animal testing but uses animal-derived materials without tracing their origins.

    The lack of transparency in tracing these materials affects their rating.
